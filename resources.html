Some tips for successful mentorship relationships:

[Goals] Agree on goals and definitions of progress at the beginning of the process

[Commitment] Agree on commitment in advance

[Session Formats] It can be traditional sessions, but also mentors can organise study groups

[Progress] Track your progress using tools you like (spreadsheets, trello, notion) and share this document

[Community] Join our Slack space to hear more about mentorship related events and ask questions if you have any
